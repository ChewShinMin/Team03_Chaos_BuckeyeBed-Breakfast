01——About Us

Experience Breathtaking Views and Warm Hospitality at London

Buckeye Bed & Breakfast is nestled in the charming village of Charlton Kings, in the suburb of Cheltenham with 11 forested acres, beautiful landscaping and stunning views. Your experience starts with a spacious room, a large comfortable bed, and a spectacular gourmet breakfast. With advanced notice we will try to accommodate any dietary restrictions. After your busy day, enjoy a complimentary evening snack and beverage to help you relax before bedtime.You can enjoy edible landscaping in season, which include blueberry, strawberry, blackberry, raspberry, apple and pear fruit trees at our inn.

02——ROOMS

ROOMS & SUITES

The Guest House of our Buckeye Bed and Breakfast offers 4 guest rooms. Each room is furnished uniquely with reproductions of American 18th century and Shaker furniture hand-built by David T. Smith.Fireplaces and/or private porches in some of the rooms of this Ohio Amish Country inn help create the perfect setting for a romantic vacation. Many rooms also feature trundle beds.
